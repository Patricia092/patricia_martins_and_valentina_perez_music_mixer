INFORMATION


Welcome to the Super Mario Music Mixer!

This is a fun project themed after the beloved Super Mario franchise. We have created an interactive music mixer where you can place several of the Mario characters on a stage to play a musical instrument! These characters include Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach, Toad, and Bowser. Each character will play an audio loop with their instrument which can all be combined to create a song!

If it is your first time using our music mixer, please click on instructions for details on how it works and its features.

INSTRUCTIONS


The Super Mario Music Mixer uses drag and drop functionality. Each of the instrument icons at the bottom of the page are draggable and can be dropped into the bordered area above it.

To get started, drag one of the instruments and drop it into the corresponding area. Upon dropping the instrument, a character will appear with the instrument that you chose and they will begin playing an audio loop. As you begin adding more instruments, the loops will combine with each other to create a song!

You can also adjust each loop individually with the options in the box that appears below each character on hover:

- The play button will restart the audio and will also play the audio again if it has been paused
- The pause button will pause the audio
- The X button will stop the audio and remove the character from the stage
- The slider can be used to either lower or increase the volume

We hope you enjoy using the Super Mario Music Mixer!
